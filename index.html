My name is Mika Ben Amar
I am based in Berlin——My email is i@cant.download

I think a lot about what it means to spend most of my time online,
and how that shapes the kind of work I make.
My practice responds to the systems that define the digital
space—how content is delivered, how interactions are framed,
how attention is tracked.
It’s less about representing those conditions, and more about
working from within them—participating in the same online
systems while shifting their function, exploring how the internet
functions as both medium and environment.
